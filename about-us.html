ABOUT TURKS

TURKS is a food company owned and operated by ZENAROSA FOOD CORPORATION, headed by MR. GEM L. ZEÑAROSA as President and CEO. TURKS started in 2007 as a food cart in SM City Sta. Mesa Food court in Quezon City. It is engaged in selling mainly BEEF PITA WRAP, freshly cooked and prepared upon order. Since then, Turks has grown into more than six hundred (600) branches nationwide as of April 2022.


Currently, Turks is ranked as the top brand among players in the same industry, recording an overwhelming amount of annual sales. TURKS is a total game changer in the industry, setting high standards in the way Pita Wraps are being prepared and served. In addition to this, TURKS takes pride in its elegant and modern-looking stores, separating itself from other brands in the industry.

Our Values

  • Quality: We use only the freshest and highest quality ingredients in all our products.
  • Customer Satisfaction: Our customers are at the heart of everything we do. We strive to exceed their expectations with every visit.
  • Innovation: We are constantly exploring new ways to improve our menu and service.
  • Community: We believe in giving back to the community and supporting local initiatives.
